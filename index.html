The Role

🎯

Loyalty Platform Product Ownership

LJI wants someone who understands loyalty at a product level — not just as a feature category but as a revenue-generating growth engine. GRAVTY® is an AI-first platform; the PM needs to understand how personalisation, engagement mechanics, and CLV optimisation work in practice, at enterprise scale.

🤖

AI-First Product Capabilities

Developing AI-first features and experiences is a core responsibility, not a stretch goal. LJI explicitly asks for hands-on LLM, AI agent, or ML delivery experience in production — not theoretical awareness. This is a technical product role that happens to need strong strategy too.

📐

Documentation & Delivery Discipline

LJI lists PRDs, user stories, functional specs, release notes, GTM plans, and success metrics — all by name. This signals a high-documentation culture where product artifacts are the currency of alignment across Engineering, Design, Sales, and CS.

📊

Data-Driven Adoption & Retention

Monitor performance using data and analytics to improve customer adoption, engagement, and retention — this is ongoing product responsibility, not a one-time launch metric. The PM owns the numbers and iterates against them continuously.

🌐

Enterprise Cross-Functional Leadership

Working across Engineering, QA, UX, AI/ML, Sales, Marketing, and Customer Success simultaneously. Enterprise SaaS means long feedback loops and many stakeholders — the PM needs to be the connective tissue that keeps all of them moving toward the same outcome.

🧭

Team Mentoring & Practice Building

LJI explicitly asks for 2–3 years of experience mentoring PMs and leading cross-functional teams. This is a senior, practice-shaping role — someone who elevates the whole team's product thinking, not just delivers their own roadmap.
